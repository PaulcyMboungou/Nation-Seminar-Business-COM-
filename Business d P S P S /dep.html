Department of Commerce & Management

The Department of Commerce was established in the year 2002 offering courses in B.Com Generals & Computers. It has been offering B.Com Vocational since 2008 focusing on Advertising, Marketing & Salesmanship. In 2011, BBA was added to the existing courses. The Department aims at equipping the students with the knowledge, skills and tools needed to face the challenges and competition in the global environment today. The Faculty Members of the Department specialized in various aspects of Commerce and Management hone the academic skills of the students, emphasizing on practical exposure through projects, seminars and workshops by inviting experts from both industry and academia. The Department has trained thousands of Graduates till date who have emerged successful in various fields.
