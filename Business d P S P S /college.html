St. Mary’s College

St. Mary’s College is a self – financing Christian Minority Institution affiliated to Osmania University. The College is run by St. Mary’s Educational Society that was established in 1982. The College started off as a Junior College first and then the Degree College was established in the year 2002. The college believes in grooming the personality of the student, not just adding qualification to the resume. It is one of the most happening Colleges in the twin cities with excellent infrastructure and facilities. It attracts students not only from other States of India but also from other Countries. Keeping with the changing times over the years, the Institution has adopted innovative academic practices that cater to the diverse needs of the students. The Management leaves no stone unturned to give the best to the students.
